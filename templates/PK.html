Pink Knight

The Pink Knight In Castle Crashers Is A DLC Character Whose Magical Attacks Are
Classed As Non-Elemental. The Pink Knight's Abilities Consists Of 5 Different Moves
Which Is Their Spalsh Attack, Magic Projectile, Air Projectile, Elemental Infusion And
Magic Jump. The Splash Attack Is A Row Of Rainbows That Causes Enemies To Perform
A Peace Sign. Their Magic Projectile Throws A Penguin Or A Pink Hamster At The
Enemies, Air Projectile Is Exactly The Same As The Splash Attack Except For The Fact Its
Done In The Air. Elemental Infusion Pierces The Enemies Armour And Does 2x Damage.
Magic Jump Makes A Pink Weasel Appear Out Of The Ground And Boost The Player Into The Air.
