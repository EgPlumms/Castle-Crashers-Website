Grey Knight

The Grey Knight In Castle Crashers Is Unlocked By Completing The Levels From Home Castle To
Barbarian Boss Whose Magical Attacks Are Classed As Non-Elemental And Fire. The Grey Knight's
Abilities Consists Of 5 Different Moves Which Is Their Spalsh Attack, Magic Projectile, Air Projectile,
Elemental Infusion And Magic Jump. The Splash Attack Causes Arrows To Rain From The Sky In Front Of
The Character In Groups Of 3, This Also Causes Enemies To Be Knocked Over. Their Magic Projectile
Throws A Bomb At The Enemies Causing Fire Damage, Air Projectile Is Exactly The Same As The Splash
Attack Except For The Fact Its Done In The Air. Elemental Infusion Is One Of The Most Basic Attack
Combos And Has No Effects At All. The Grey Knight's Magic Jump Makes The Character Leap Into The Air
With A Dust Effect That Damages Overlaps Enemies.
