Hatty

Hatty In Castle Crashers Is Unlocked By Either Owning BattleBlock Theatre Completing
An Achievement In BattleBlock Theatre Or Getting A DLC Whose Magical Attacks Are
Classed As Non-Elemental. Hatty's Abilities Consists Of 5 Different Moves Which Is Their
Spalsh Attack, Magic Projectile, Air Projectile, Elemental Infusion And Magic Jump. The
Splash Attack Makes Cubes Of Gold Fall From The Sky In Sets Of Four Each Set Is Accompanied By A Golden Whale That Swoops In And Knocks Enemies Over. Their Magic Projectile Makes A Beam Of Tears Shoot From Hatty's Eyes. Hatty's Air Projectile Throws A Giant Green Gem To Be Thrown To The Ground. Elemental Infusion Pierces The Enemies Armour And Does 2x Damage. Hatty's Magic Jump Gives Hatty A Pair Of White Wings As He Leaps Up.
