Red Knight

The Red Knight In Castle Crashers Is Known For Its Electrical Abilities.
The Red Knight's Abilities Consists Of 5 Different Moves Which Is Their Spalsh Attack, Magic Projectile, Air Projectile, Elemental Infusion And Magic Jump. The Splash Attack Is A Lightning Bolt
That Slowly Drains MP Until Its Thrown At An Enemy. Their Magic Projectile Is A Small Ball Of Electric
Sparks The Air Projectile Is Exactly The Same As This Except For The Fact Its Done In The Air. The Red
Knight's Elemental Infusion Electrifies Their Sword Stunning The Enemies They Hit With It. Magic Jump
Is Just A Little Trail/Tower Of Electricity That Follows You As You Jump While Using Magic That Damages The Enemies.
