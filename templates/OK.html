Orange Knight

The Orange Knight In Castle Crashers Is Knows For Its Fire Abilities. The Orange Knight's Abilities
Consists Of 5 Different Moves Which Is Their Spalsh Attack, Magic Projectile, Air Projectile, Elemental
Infusion And Magic Jump. The Orange Knight's Spalsh Attack Creates A Horizontal Burst Of Flames
That Knocksbacks Enemies And Does Significant Damage. Their Magical Projetile Is A Ball Of Flames
That Can Either Be Shot Horizontally Or Downwards, Air Projectile Is The Exact Same Thing Just Shot
Mid Air And Not On The Ground. Their Elemental Infusion Engulfs The Enemies In Flames. Lastly, Their
Magic Jump Creates A Pillar Of Flames Lighting Any Enemies Close Enough On Fire.
