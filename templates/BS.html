The BlackSmith

BlackSmith

The Blacksmith In Castle Crashers Is A DLC Character, His Elemental Abilities Are Fire
And Non-Elemental. The Blacksmith's Abilities Consists Of 5 Different Moves Which Is
Their Spalsh Attack, Magic Projectile, Air Projectile, Elemental Infusion And Magic Jump.
The Splash Attack Makes A Frog Appear That Will Appear Behind The Player And Stick
Its Tongue Out Damaging Any Enemies It Hits. Their Magic Projectile Is A Flaming
Hammer. The Air Projectile Is Exactly The Same As The Splash Attack Except For The
Frog Appears Below The Player This Time. The BlackSmith's Elemental Infusion Pierces
The Enemies Armour And Does 2x Damage. Magic Jump Makes A SpringBoard Appear
That Damages Enemies Near It And Throws The Character In The Air.
