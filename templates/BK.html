Blue Knight

The Blue Knight In Castle Crashers Is Known For Its Ice Abilities. The Blue Knight's
Abilities Consists Of 5 Different Moves Which Is Their Spalsh Attack, Magic Projectile, Air
Projectile, Elemental Infusion And Magic Jump. The Splash Attack Is A Row Of Icicles
Falling Onto The Enemy Freezing Them. Their Magic Projectile Is A Small Ball Of Ice That
Freezes The Enemy, Air Projectile Is Exactly The Same As The Splash Attack Except For
The Fact Its Done In The Air. Elemental Infusion Is Just Using Magic To Make It When You
Hit The Enemies With Your Sword They Freeze. Magic Jump Is Just A Small Tower Of Ice
That Appears When Jumping.
